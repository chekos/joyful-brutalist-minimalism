# 0005 — Principles speak about human outcomes

- Status: accepted
- Date: 2026-07-17
- Issue: https://github.com/chekos/joyful-brutalist-minimalism/issues/35
- Owning authority: `DESIGN.md`

## Context

The first reference site made several principles sound like descriptions of
the repository, Figma, or a technical implementation. "Preserve an escape
hatch" framed access as a way out of the intended experience. "Animate
explanation" described what motion can reveal without saying why a person
would benefit. The inspectability principle emphasized indexes and measures
instead of traceability. The one-delight rule limited effects without
explaining why surprise loses its force when everything performs.

The page repeated those abstractions through lens glyphs, counts, category
labels, and a spacing specimen. The result contradicted the same principles:
geometry and technical language appeared without enough work to do.

## Decision

The principles now name human outcomes:

- inspectability means that important information is traceable;
- joy is useful work;
- delight remains effective by staying unexpected;
- animation deepens understanding but never carries the only meaning;
- each medium adapts shared intent to what it does well; and
- access is designed from the start for different people, devices,
  connections, and ways of interacting.

Tool-specific reconciliation remains in the authority and sync contracts. The
reference site removes redundant lens encoding and ordinary infrastructure
specimens when they do not help a reader understand or use the language.

## Consequences

The constitution can be read as a general design philosophy without knowing
the repository toolchain. The reference site must demonstrate the principles
through clear hierarchy, realistic content, and restrained information
geometry rather than narrating its implementation.

The 4px space scale remains portable infrastructure, but the page does not
present a standard token ladder as an authored visual idea. Figma and browser
studies should remove classifications or marks that have no job beyond
repeating nearby text.
